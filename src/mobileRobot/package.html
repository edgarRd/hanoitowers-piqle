!DOCTYPE HTML PUBLIC "-//W3C//DTD HTML 3.2 Final//EN"> Racing car A car, inside a circuit, try to keep inside it, and to drive as fast as possible. It can have a sight of its environment through a linear retina : each pixel of this retina indicates whether a wall is near, not so far, or very far. One can modify the size of this retina, the angle of vision, the maximum speed of the car.
